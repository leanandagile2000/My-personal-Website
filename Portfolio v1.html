§ 04 — Principles

What I care about as a program leader.

i.
Clarity is the deliverable.
The program plan isn't the output — understanding is. My job is to make sure every stakeholder knows what we're doing, why, what's at risk, and what they need to decide. When that's true, execution takes care of itself.
ii.
Surface problems early. Loudly.
The cost of hiding a risk is always higher than the cost of naming it. I build cultures where teams feel safe escalating, and where bad news travels fast — not because we panic, but because we act.
iii.
The lightest process that works.
Process is overhead until it isn't. I use the minimum structure that keeps a team aligned and moving — and I cut ruthlessly when the scaffold becomes the ceiling.
